Description:
Council roadworks contractor struck CONDUIT-SYD-MEL-INLAND with a mechanical excavator near Campbelltown at GPS -34.0650, 150.8140. The excavator bucket scuffed the outer conduit sheath but did not breach the inner duct or damage any fibre strands. A field inspector was dispatched within 90 minutes and confirmed superficial scuffing only — no fibre damage, no signal degradation, no service impact. All optical telemetry on LINK-SYD-MEL-FIBRE-01 remained completely stable throughout the incident window.

Detection Method:
Third-party notification — council works supervisor called the infrastructure hotline after the excavator operator reported hitting a marked conduit. No network-side alerts were generated because no optical or electrical fault occurred.

Resolution:
Field inspection team dispatched at 12:15. Inspector arrived at 13:45 and confirmed conduit outer sheath scuffing (approx. 30cm x 5cm area). Inner duct intact, all fibre strands verified undamaged via visual inspection and OTDR trace. Conduit repair sleeve installed as preventive measure. Contractor issued a near-miss report. No customer impact, no service interruption, no rerouting required.
