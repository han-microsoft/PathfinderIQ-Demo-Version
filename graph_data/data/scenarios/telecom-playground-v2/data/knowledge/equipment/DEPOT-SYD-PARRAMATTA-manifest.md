## Notes

- Parramatta depot services metro Sydney exchange sites and serves as a staging point for Sydney-end DWDM corridor work.
- This depot does NOT directly service amplifier sites on the Sydney–Melbourne backbone. Amplifier site dispatch is routed through Campbelltown (south) or the depot network coordinator.
- Extended operating hours reflect metro-area SLA commitments (4-hour response for Priority 1 faults within the Sydney metro zone).
